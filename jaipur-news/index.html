About Jaipur

Jaipur, popularly known as the Pink City, is the capital of the Indian state of Rajasthan and one of the country's most visited destinations. Home to landmarks such as Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, the City Palace and Jantar Mantar, it blends a rich heritage with a fast-growing modern city — making timely, local news especially valuable for those who live in or follow the region.
