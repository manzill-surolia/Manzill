Property types

  • Plots & land — residential and investment plots in developing areas.
  • Independent houses & villas — for families looking for more space.
  • Commercial property — shops, offices and showrooms for businesses.

Popular Jaipur localities

Jaipur's real estate spans well-established and fast-growing neighbourhoods, including Mansarovar, Vaishali Nagar, Malviya Nagar, Jagatpura, C-Scheme, and the corridors along Ajmer Road and Tonk Road. Each area offers a different mix of connectivity, amenities and price points for buyers and renters.

Why Jaipur

As the capital of Rajasthan and a major business, education and tourism hub, Jaipur has seen steady growth in housing and infrastructure. Improving connectivity and a rising population make it an active market for both end-users and investors.
